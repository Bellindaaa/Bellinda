Services

Custom Design Consultation

Offering one-on-one consultations to explore clients' individual style, vision, and preferences. Together, we brainstorm and sketch ideas to bring unique designs to life, resulting in pieces that reflect the client's personality and aesthetic.

Made-to-Measure Garments

Creating clothing with impeccable fit by tailoring each piece to the client's exact measurements. This service ensures not only comfort but also a flawless silhouette, bringing high-quality craftsmanship and personalization to every garment.

Collection Development

Comprehensive design and production services for clients seeking a full fashion collection. This includes concept creation, mood boards, fabric and material sourcing, and overseeing production to deliver a cohesive and impactful collection from start to finish.

Styling and Wardrobe Curation

Providing expert styling advice to help clients build a versatile, cohesive wardrobe. This service includes personal styling tips, pairing suggestions, and curating new pieces that complement existing wardrobes, making it easier for clients to dress with confidence.

Trend Forecasting

Analyzing market and industry trends to guide design choices that align with upcoming fashion shifts. Ideal for brands or clients looking to stay ahead of the curve, this service helps in planning seasonal updates or new product lines that resonate with modern tastes.

Alterations and Garment Care

Offering post-purchase alterations to perfect fit, as well as professional guidance on garment care. This service helps clients maintain the quality and longevity of their pieces, ensuring each item stays as beautiful and wearable as the day it was made.

Soft skill

Creativity

The ability to conceptualize unique and original designs that reflect current trends or create new ones. Creativity helps a designer push boundaries and deliver innovative, stylish, and standout pieces that capture attention.

Communication

Clear communication is essential for explaining design concepts to clients, collaborating with teams, and coordinating with manufacturers. A designer needs to effectively convey ideas and listen to feedback to ensure the final product meets expectations.

Attention to Detail

Fashion design requires a focus on even the smallest elements, from stitching and fabric choices to fit and finishing. Designers must ensure that every detail, whether visible or not, contributes to the overall quality and aesthetics of the garment.

Hard skill

Pattern Making

Pattern making involves transforming design sketches into actual patterns that will be used to cut and sew fabric. A designer must know how to create and adjust patterns for the best fit and silhouette, ensuring the final garment aligns with the design concept. \

Sewing and Garment Construction

This skill involves both machine and hand-sewing techniques to construct garments from start to finish. Designers must understand the basics of garment construction, from stitching seams to adding finishing touches like hems or embellishments.

Fashion Design Software

Knowledge of tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, or specialized fashion design software is essential for creating digital sketches, tech packs, and 3D garment models. These tools help designers visualize, modify, and present their designs more efficiently.
